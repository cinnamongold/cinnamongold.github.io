How to make banana bread

Banana bread is a very flavorful bread with the flavor of a tasty banana and scrumptious bread! In this tutorial, I am going to show you how to make YOUR OWN BANANA BREAD!!!!!!!!!! Keep reading to learn more :))))))))))))))))

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tspn baking soda
  • 1/2 tspn salt
  • 1 stick unsalted and softened butter
  • 3/4 - 1 cup sugar (brown or granulated)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3-4 very ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 tspn vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F. Grease a 9x5 in. loaf pan
  2. In a bowl, mash bananas until smooth
  3. In a separate large bowl, cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy
  4. Add eggs to the butter mixture, beating well after each. Mix in mashed bananas and vanilla extract
  5. In another bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt
  6. Gently mix the dry ingredients into the wet mixture, don't overmix
  7. Fold in your chocolate chips
  8. Pour the batter into your prepared loaf pan
  9. Bake for 50-65 minutes
  10. Let the bread cool for about 10-15 minutes, then enjoy!!!!!
